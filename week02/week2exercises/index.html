My Favorite Superheroes

BATMAN!

The worlds greatest detective, Bruce Wayne masquerades as a symbol of fear for Gotham's criminal underbelly, enforcing vigilante justice motivated by the murder of his parents while grappling with crippling personality disfunction and lingering trauma.

The Tick

The Tick is a satirical superhero created by Ben Edlund. The Tick is most famously known (hopefully) for the 90s cartoon syndication, which abandoned some of the source materials darker tones and sexual innuendo for a focus on absurdist superhero parody and campy comedy.

Hellboy

Hellboy is an irreverent, cigar-chomping, but ultimately well-meaning half-demon in the employ of the Bureau for Paranormal Research and Defense (BPRD). Hellboy explores occultist themes and questions of self and identity, as Hellboy consistently quite literally files off his demon horns in a rejection of his own nature.
